11. Location Data Collection and Use

In Short: We collect and use location data to provide location-based services and improve app functionality.

What Location Data We Collect

Our GRA MTS App collects the following types of location information:

  • Precise Location: GPS coordinates when you enable location services
  • Approximate Location: General area based on IP address or network information
  • Location History: Previous locations visited while using the app
  • Movement Patterns: Routes and travel patterns for service optimization

How We Use Location Data

Service Delivery

Find nearby tax offices, provide directions, estimate travel times

App Functionality

Location-based notifications, regional content, service availability

Analytics

Improve service coverage, optimize office locations, usage patterns

Security

Fraud prevention, account security, suspicious activity detection

Location Data Controls

You can control location data collection through:

  • Device Settings: Enable/disable location services for our app
  • App Permissions: Grant or revoke location access in app settings
  • Precision Level: Choose between precise and approximate location sharing
Note: Disabling location services may limit certain app features, such as finding nearby tax offices or receiving location-based notifications.
